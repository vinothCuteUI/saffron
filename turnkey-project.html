About Turnkey Projects

A turnkey project means handing over your entire project responsibility to us. From planning, budgeting, design, approvals, material procurement, construction, interiors, MEP, HVAC and final finishing everything is handled by Saffron’s expert team.

You only need to “turn the key” and move into your completed space.
